Ubuntu metahelp required

Date Tue 18 May 2010 Tags ubuntu

I just got back from UDS-M. If you've never been to a UDS, you probably don't know what an intense, inspiring, fun, draining time it can be.

Anyway, I got back and started mucking around on my Ubuntu 10.04 LTS laptop on my home network. Everything was great until – suddenly – the wireless connection drops out. I have two other devices (Android, OS X) that are connected just fine, so I know it's probably not a network configuration issue.
I also have noticed this behaviour all the way through Karmic and since the Lucid beta.
I want to fix this bug. I want this to be my personal contribution to Maverick. I'm inspired, I'm *pumped*, but I don't really know where to begin. I don't even know how to file a good bug about this.
That's where I need your help.
