Black out

Date Tue 26 January 2010

I've been a bit slack and have yet to black out my website or avatar to protest the Australian government's mandatory Internet censorship policy.

In case circumstances prevent me from doing so, please read the [Internet Blackout Australia](http://www.internetblackout.com.au/) website to see why it's so important. You might also want to read [Hugh Blemings' well-written thoughts](http://blemings.org/hugh/blog/blosxom.cgi/2010/01/25#20100125a) on the matter.
